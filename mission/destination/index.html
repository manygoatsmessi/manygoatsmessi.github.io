LANDING SITE

The Ares Colony will be situated in the Chryse Chaos, an area slightly above the equator near Mars' largest canyon, Valles Marineris. The landing site will be on the south-west rim of one of the craters in the area. This crater is not yet named, and is decently large with a diameter of roughly 20 kilometers. It is around 200 km west of another major crater, Crater Mojave and 200 km north-east of Da Vinci Crater.
