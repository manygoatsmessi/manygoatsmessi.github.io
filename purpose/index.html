PURPOSE

Mars is such an intriguing planet. This is because it is so similar to the Earth. With a similar axial tilt, average temperature, day length and size, there has even been evidence of liquid water on the planet's surface, many years ago. These reasons make the exploration of this world a certainty. Throughout history, multiple nations have sent multiple satellites, rovers and landers to the red planet. One thing that has not happenned yet, however, is human exploration of Mars. Ares will be the first step in this endeavor. It will allow the first ever humans to step on the surface of Mars.

WHY WE ARE GOING TO MARS

TECHNOLOGIES

The innovative ideas developed over the course of the mission will provide the technology for even more complex missions to outer solar system and beyond. Furthermore, some technologies may even be integrated into everyday life, allowing our own lives to be easier.

SCIENCE

As well as technological, Ares will also provide many scientifical advancement to mankind. These scientific breakthroughs may even allow further advanced technologies to be developed.

ECONOMY

As missions get more and more complex, the workforce must increase along with it. The Ares Program will create thousands of new jobs. Furthermore, Ares will open up and support entirely new industries.

INSPIRATION AND EDUCATION

During the Ares Program, more and more people will be inspired and educated about the wonders of our Solar System. These people will become the next generation for human space exploration,

INTERNATIONAL PEACE

As we work with our international partners to launch and progress through the Ares mission, we demonstrate peace for all mankind. Through this, we show that space travel crosses through both science and politics.

DIVERSITY AND EQUALITY

The Ares program will show that all people are equal on this planet by creating opportunites for people of all religions, races and genders to participate in this historic mission.
